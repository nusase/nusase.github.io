SASE Member of the Month:
Jenny Cheung




Congratulations to Jenny Cheung, our February Member of the month!

Why did you join SASE?
I joined SASE because of the encouragement from some upperclassmen I had met. All the members of SASE are so supportive and approachable.

Being surrounded by such caring and passionate people inspires and empowers me to venture out of my comfort zone to do things I never thought I could do.

Now, I have attended multiple conferences and had the chance to develop my networking skills. SASE pushed me even further by encouraging me to take up a leadership role by being involved in planning a conference with an awesome team!

Through SASE, I hope to continue my growth personally and professionally! #SASEFAMILY
